About Us

We’re a 501(c)(3) non-profit community-based effort organization designed to uplift our community by providing mental health support and resources.

Vision

Restoring HOPE in Alliance with each other by Empowering the community.

We all have a role to play. We invite individuals, communities, organizations, and leaders to join in our efforts to create long-lasting changes in mental health equity and to prevent suicide amongst underrepresented communities.
